Frequently Asked Questions

How do I access the Welfare Fund?
Members can apply via the Dashboard. Requests are reviewed by community leaders for emergency repatriation or medical support within 24 hours.
What is the Digital ID used for?
The Digital ID serves as community verification for sports events, SOS assistance, and exclusive member discounts at partner businesses.
How does the SOS Emergency work?
Once you join AFCODD and set up your emergency contacts, the SOS button provides one-tap alerts to both community responders and your next of kin.
